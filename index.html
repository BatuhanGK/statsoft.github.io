Dr. Aijun Zhang is currently an assistant professor in Department of Statistics of Actuarial Science at The University of Hong Kong. He received his PhD degree in Statistics from The University of Michigan in Ann Arbor in 2009, then worked in Bank of America for over five years. He later served as the R&D director of Center for Big Data in Education based in Hong Kong Baptist University, where he obtained his BSc and MPhil degrees in mathemtics and statistics. Dr. Zhang's R&D specialities range from statistics, big data to artificial intelligence.
